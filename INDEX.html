Institute for Credentialing Excellence and Lifelong Learning

The Institute for Credentialing Excellence and Lifelong Learning (iCELL) is committed to promoting knowledge in the areas of Engineering, Business, Sciences, Information Technology, Arts and Languages, and other important disciplines by establishing linkages to industry partners and accrediting agencies.

iCELL offers value-augmenting opportunities for professionals and students. The institute aims to develop the competencies of professional workers through the provision of short courses, training and seminars, and other learning and enrichment opportunities. iCELL also aspires to provide essential knowledge and skills for the successful alignment of their competencies with the industry workforce demand.

The institute, in line with the corporate mission of Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao, A Mapúa School, upholds excellent and relevant education to make its members globally competitive and locally in demand.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Be a prime institution that fosters excellent Lifelong Learning and professional development through innovative learning methodologies to significantly contribute to the development of the society.

Mission

The Institute of Credentialing Excellence and Lifelong Learning (ICELL) is committed to promote knowledge in the areas of Engineering, Business, Sciences, Information Technology, Arts and Languages, Health Sciences, and other essential disciplines by establishing linkages to industry partners and accrediting agencies.

ICELL offers value-augmenting opportunities for professionals and students. The institute aims to develop the competencies of professional workers through the provision of short courses, trainings and seminars, and other learning and enrichment opportunities. ICELL also aspires to provide undergraduate students, primarily of Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao, the essential knowledge and skills for the successful alignment of their competencies with the industry workforce demand.

The institute, in line with the corporate mission of Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao, upholds excellent and relevant education to make its members globally competitive and locally in-demand.
